## English Description

### Short (Promotional Text — 170 chars)
Clean up your camera roll in 1 minute! Swipe left = delete, right = keep. Challenge your friends — who sorts fastest? 📸

### Full Description

**Your camera roll is a mess. Zwiip makes cleanup fun.**

Swipe left to delete. Right to keep. That's it.

Zwiip turns the boring task of cleaning up photos into a fast, addictive experience. Think Tinder — but for your photos.

**⚡ SORT IN 1 MINUTE**
No complicated menus. Just swipe. Most users sort 40-60 photos per minute.

**🛡️ ZWIIP SAFE**
Enable Safe Timer (0.5-3 sec) to prevent accidental deletes. See each photo properly before deciding.

**🏆 CHALLENGE YOUR FRIENDS**
Start a 1-minute Challenge and see how many photos you can sort. Share your score and challenge friends — who's the fastest?

**📊 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS**
• Photos sorted, MB freed, swipes per minute
• Achievements and milestones
• Session and lifetime stats

**📁 SORT INTO ALBUMS**
Swipe up or down to move photos directly into albums. Configure what each direction does.

**↩️ ALWAYS UNDO**
Deleted by mistake? Tap undo — or restore from the Trashed screen. Nothing is permanently deleted without your confirmation.

**🌍 15 LANGUAGES**
Automatic based on your phone language. Danish, English, German, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic and Hindi.

Free up space. Clean up the mess. Zwiip it away. 📸
